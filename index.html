02 — About

I design growth, identity and AI-powered consumer experiences — and I care more about the problem than the pixels.

Across Microsoft Edge, Windows, Microsoft Search and automotive platforms, I've spent 11+ years turning complex, high-stakes problems into experiences people actually understand. Today I lead Growth Design for Edge — onboarding, activation, retention and personalization for millions of customers.

Before that, I shaped identity and authentication for billions of Windows sign-ins, advancing Microsoft's passwordless future. I work where product strategy, experimentation, systems thinking and AI innovation meet — partnering closely with Product, Engineering, Research, Data Science and Content.
